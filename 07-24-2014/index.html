A proof of concept collapsing panel layout

As a way to represent hierarchical information, the layout expands a given "module", showcasing the details of that module, into ever-expanding panels. This allows for diagrams that represent large chunks of information as layers, but in an easy to navigate way. Click each box to expand its panel.
